My main BOOK OF THE MONTH

Hangwoman BY K.R.MEERA

Synopsis

"Hangwoman" is a dark and haunting thriller set in a remote village in the misty mountains of Himachal Pradesh, India. In this chilling tale, tradition clashes with modernity, and the mysteries of the past intertwine with the complexities of the present.

Chetna Grddha Mullick, a strong-willed young woman, is destined to inherit the unusual and macabre family profession as the village's hangwoman. Her forebears have been executing criminals for generations, earning them a mix of respect and fear in the tight-knit community.
